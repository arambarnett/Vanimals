**Mitigation:**
- Pilot programs with measurable ROI

- Social login (Google, Apple) ‚Üí instant wallet

**Risk:** Larger players copy the model
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage (network effects)
- Real integrations (Strava, Plaid) = barrier
- Community ownership (token holders invested)
- Continuous innovation (seasonal drops, new categories)

# A10: FAQs for Investors

### Q: Is the product actually live?
**A:** Yes. iOS app is in the App Store (v1.0.5), backend is deployed, Strava integration is functional, and NFTs mint on Aptos blockchain. This is not vaporware.

### Q: How do you compete with Duolingo?
**A:** We're not competing directly. Duolingo owns language learning; we're multi-category (fitness, finance, education, etc.) with true NFT ownership and brand integrations. Complementary, not competitive.

### Q: Why blockchain? Isn't Web3 dead?
**A:** Blockchain enables true ownership and portability. Users own their Sprouts (can trade, display, take to other platforms). Aptos has no gas fees, so UX is seamless. We hide the complexity‚Äîusers just see "collectible characters."

### Q: What if users don't pay?
**A:** Multiple revenue streams: IAP, brand sponsorships, subscriptions, tokens. Even if only 10-15% of users pay (industry standard), brands subsidize acquisition through sponsored challenges.

### Q: How do you prevent cheating (fake Strava activities)?
**A:** Strava has anti-cheating mechanisms (GPS verification, speed limits). We can add additional fraud detection (ML models, velocity checks) and require manual review for high-value rewards.

### Q: What's the regulatory risk with tokens?
**A:** We're structuring $SPROUT as a utility token (not security). It's used for in-app rewards and governance, not investment. We'll work with legal counsel to ensure Howey Test compliance.

### Q: Can I try the app now?
**A:** Yes! Search "Sprouts" in the iOS App Store or contact us for TestFlight access.
